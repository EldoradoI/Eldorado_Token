Eldorado Token (ELD)

I'm an ordinary guy from Ukraine, working as a shipbuilding installer in Poland. I've fallen and stood back up many times — with no money, no support, just belief in myself.

I created Eldorado Token not to get rich quick, but as a symbol of strength, dignity, and self-growth — for people like me. If you've kept going when it was hard, this token is for you.

Right now, ELD exists as a free token on Binance Smart Chain. In the future, I plan to give it more meaning — maybe discounts, access to content, airdrops, or even its own community.

This isn't about hype. It's about the path of a regular person building his own Eldorado.
